# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Mataram Dev community and this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

**Behavior that contributes to a positive environment includes:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Behavior that is not acceptable includes:**

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing another person's private information (such as a physical or email address) without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including the GitHub repository, issue tracker, pull requests, and any community communication channels (Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp, etc.). It also applies when an individual is representing the project or community in public settings.

---

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers at:

**Email:** contact@mataramdev.org

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the identity of the reporter.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these guidelines when determining consequences for violations:

### 1. Correction

**Impact:** Minor, isolated incident.  
**Response:** A private written warning with clarification of the violation and a request to correct behavior.

### 2. Warning

**Impact:** A single significant violation or pattern of minor violations.  
**Response:** A formal warning with specified consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved for a defined period.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Impact:** A serious violation of community standards.  
**Response:** Temporary ban from all project spaces for a defined period. No public or private interaction with the people involved during this period.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Impact:** Demonstrated pattern of violations, sustained harassment, or severe misconduct.  
**Response:** Permanent removal from all project spaces.
