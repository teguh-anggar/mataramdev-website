About Community

We started as a small group of friends who wanted to learn coding together. Now we’re a growing community of tech enthusiasts — from high college students to professional developers — who meet regularly to share knowledge, build projects, and support each other.

Our mission: Make a place where anyone can start their journey and grow as a professional in technology, whatever their background and experience.

Our Activities

💻

WFC (Work From Cafe)

Every Wednesday we gather to work on open-source or personal projects, solve coding challenges, or just learn something new together while enjoying a cup of coffee.

🎤

Meetup & Sharing Sessions

Monthly meetups where members share their experiences and knowledge in a relaxed environment, build networks, and connect with like-minded individuals from all acrross backgrounds.

🚀

Project Colab

Got an idea? Find teammates here. We regularly kick off small group projects so everyone can build a real portofolio (Check Our Github).

🎓

Course & Language Center

We run tech courses and language classes for members who want to learn new skills. From web development to English & Japanese, we got you covered.

🛠️

Workshop

We organize hands-on workshops to help members develop practical skills in various technologies and tools.

📚

Book Club

We also have a book club where members read and discuss books related to technology, personal development, and entrepreneurship.

🏆

Competitions

We organize coding competitions and hackathons to challenge members to apply their skills, learn from each other, and win prizes.

🎮

Fun & Games

We also organize fun activities like game nights, quizes, and social events to keep the community spirit alive and strong.
